Frequently asked questions

How quickly can I set up my job board using Job Boardly?

Job Boardly is designed for quick and easy setup. In just a few minutes, you can have your job board ready to go with our intuitive dashboard.

Can I customize the appearance of my job board to match my brand?

Absolutely! Job Boardly offers easy customization options, allowing you to personalize the look and feel of your job board, including logo, colors, and branding elements.

Can I connect my own domain?

Absolutely! Job Boardly empowers you to seamlessly connect your own domain to your job board. The process is quick and easy, ensuring that your job board reflects your brand identity effortlessly. If you have any questions or need assistance during the domain connection process, our support team is here to help.

Is there a limit to the number of job postings or users on Job Boardly?

With Job Boardly, there are no limits or caps the number of job postings or job alerts. Enjoy the freedom to scale your job board without restrictions.

How can I backfill jobs on my board using Job Boardly?

Job Boardly provides an automatic backfill feature, allowing you to enrich your job board with relevant content from various sources. Customize the backfill based on category, country, and keyword preferences. You can use either of our best-in-class backfilling tools; the Magic Aggregator or the Turbo Backfiller.
